What is WEXPLORE3D?

Wexplore3D is a dedicated platform to share and sell your 3D designs which connects designers, 3d printers and people who want to 3D print objects. We aregrowing community with inspiring 3D designers and enthusiast. Wexplore3D.com works as a social network platform that connects all people who are involved in 3D printing and designing which helps to interact with each other.
